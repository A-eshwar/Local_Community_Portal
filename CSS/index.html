About Our Portal

This lightweight, browser-based portal was created to make it easy for residents to stay connected with their community and never miss out on important local events.

Whether you're looking to volunteer, attend social gatherings, or learn about community initiatives, this portal is your one-stop destination.

Community Bulletin

Summer Festival Schedule Released

The city council has released the official schedule for the upcoming Summer Festival. Highlights include the Community Potluck, local bands performing in the evening, and a children's parade scheduled for the first weekend in July. Volunteers are still needed for setup and cleanup.

Road Closure on Main Street

Main Street will be partially closed next week for essential repairs. Please follow posted detours and allow extra travel time when attending events near City Hall. The Public Works Department thanks residents for their patience.

Volunteer Training Sessions

Volunteer training sessions for event staff and coordinators are scheduled throughout June. Sessions cover safety protocols, customer service, and logistics. Interested residents can sign up via the registration form.
